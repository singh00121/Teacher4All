How it works for Students?

You just need to post your requirement and relevant teachers will contact you within few hours If you don’t wish to post your requirement, you can search for teachers relevant for you and then get contact of the tutors using coins.

Can I post Assignment help?

You can post any kind of study help requirement including Tutoring requirement, Assignment help, Homework help or doubt solving session.

How much do I need to pay to post a requirement?

Zero. It’s totally free for you. No need to even add any card or payment details. It’s absolutely free. Just post your requirement and get contacted by best Tutors across the world for free.

What happens when any tutor contact me?

You need to discuss the terms with teacher, remuneration and the payment method. We don’t charge anything from the remuneration you will be paying to Tutor. We recommend you to request for demo session from the Tutor.

What if I am not satisfied with any of the Tutors who contacted me?

You need to repost the same project and fresh set of Tutors will get in touch with you. That being said, we only select quality Tutors on our platform. We remove many tutors from our platform due to misconduct or other quality issues.

If you have any further doubts, you can post your doubt on “Students discussion Forum” or email us at Nishant@findurtutor.com  

How it works for Tutors?

Once you completed your teaching profile, you can check the learning requirements posted by the students on the site and just contact students directly.

What are the charges I need to pay to get contact details of student?

You can contact many students for free. For others, you will need to buy the coins and use some coins to get the contact of the student. Nevertheless the charges for getting the contact of students are very less since our main intention is to help students and create a healthy tutoring ecosystem.

Can Student contact me using the site?

Yes. Students can also contact you by finding your profile from the website.

What will be the charges if student contacts me?

There will be no charges if you are directly contacted by the student.

Why do I have to pay to contact students?

We wanted to keep the site completely free for Tutors also. However, we want only relevant Tutors to contact the students and we don’t want students to get flooded with tons of messages. In order to limit the number of Tutors contacting the students and to avoid spamming for students, we have kept a minor fees to get contact of the students.

Who decides the coins required to contact a student?

Tutors decide. Coins required to contact a lead depends on the competition amongst the Tutors. Posts with low competition become free after some time and. High competition leads to an increase in price. Starting price is based on competition of similar past posts.

What happens after I contact a student?

You need to discuss the terms with student, remuneration and the payment method. We don’t charge anything from the remuneration you will be receiving. We recommend you to offer free demo session to the student to convince him/her.

What if the student doesn't respond when I contact them?

Student may not response due to many reasons including the below:

  1. student may have already found another teacher.
  2. student didn't find your initial message worthy of a reply.
  3. The student checked your profile and didn’t find you relevant..
  4. The student is just not checking the messages for any number of reasons.

If the student doesn't have a verified phone number and even doesn'tcheck your message you sent using our site within 15 days, we give a full refund for the coins used to send that message. Once refunded, coins won't be deducted again even if the student sees your message after 15 days.

If a student sees your message but doesn't reply, then no refund is given. Also, when student has a verified phone number, no refund is provided since the contact is made using phone number. However, we always provide a refund in case the job posted is found to be a scam/spam/mistake.

What if I can't pay for coins?

You can still benefit from our site in any of the following way:

  1. You can apply for free jobs for which demand is less or has fallen over the period.
  2. Wait for students to contact you (free for you).
  3. See other options like referrals to get coins for free without paying for them
  4. You can bid for free (in this you cannot send message to student) but student can contact you by spending his/her coins if he/she finds your profile relevant
Do coins expire?

Coins don't expire, ever.

How do I get paid?

Teachers deal directly with students or mediators. So you decide the amount and payment mode with your students/clients
